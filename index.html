CONTACT ME BY EMAIL

Peter McKinnon is an internationally acclaimed photographer, filmmaker, YouTube creator and entrepreneur based in Toronto, Canada. Peter uses his platforms to inspire and help people pursue their passions behind the lens.

In 2019 he was named Breakout YouTuber of the Year at the Shorty Awards and in 2020 received a Streamy for Cinematographer of the Year. Peter’s work can be found in a number of outlets but a notable personal achievement for this Canadian was having his work featured on two special collection coins issued by the Royal Canadian Mint.

It’s true that photography as a hobby is expensive. However, if you pursue it professionally as i did, you’ll have a lot of potential earnings just by simply doing the thing you love – taking photos.
